Dr. Ravi Kiran Gautam

Throughout my Post Graduate period, I have gained extensive clinical experience and honed valuable skills in various aspects of patient care. In addition to proficiently managing wards and providing pre- and post-operative care, I have excelled in active listening, promptly addressing patient concerns, delivering counseling, offering reassurance, and facilitating timely patient discharge. Moreover, my surgical expertise spans a wide range of urologic procedures, including nephrectomies, pyeloplasty, renal transplantation, lithotripsy, ureteric interventions, cystoscopy, prostate surgeries, and various other interventions. These experiences have equipped me with the technical proficiency, knowledge, and compassion needed to provide exceptional care to patients while continuously expanding my clinical repertoire.
